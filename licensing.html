Licensing FAQ

What's the difference between Trial and Pro?

The Free Trial provides exactly the same technical capabilities as the Pro version, restricted to 14 days and a single user for evaluation.

What happens if I change my computer?

Pro keys are bound to your hardware. If you upgrade your PC, you can easily deactivate the old instance in your dashboard or contact support.

What if I reinstall Windows?

As long as the hardware stays exactly the same, the OS reinstall will not invalidate your hardware ID.

How does the Renewal Key work?

Since Pro is a perpetual license, you keep the version you bought forever. The renewal key just gives you another 12 months of major software updates.
