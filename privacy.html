Privacy Policy

SQLSkrypter operates with a 100% offline, privacy-first architecture.

At SQLSkrypter, we fundamentally believe that your database architecture, server names, and execution scripts are proprietary business secrets. Therefore, SQLSkrypter was designed from the ground up to never transmit any data outside of your local machine.

Key Privacy Tenets:

  • Zero Telemetry: We do not track application usage, error logs, or execution statistics.
  • Local Storage: All configurations, connection strings, and server credentials are encrypted exclusively on your hardware using Windows DPAPI.
  • No Cloud Synchronization: Your session states and application configurations remain entirely local.
